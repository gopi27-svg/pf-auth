📱 How to get your Face Authentication Screenshot from Umang App

  1. Open the Umang App on your mobile phone.
  2. Login with your UAN Number and password.
  3. Go to EPFO → select "Employee Centric Services".
  4. Tap on "Face Authentication" and complete the face scan.
  5. Once completed, you will see a Success Screen — take a screenshot of this screen and upload it above.
